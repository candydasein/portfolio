Michael Loren Siegel

Full Stack Web Developer

I am a full stack web developer with a background in higher education, strategic communications, and social entrepreneurship. I love nothing more than solving problems, learning and growing, bringing people together, and helping make others' lives more fulfilling. I have 17 years of professional experience as a professor and scholar of film and digital media studies, an institutional reformer in the area of diversity, inclusion, and equity, a writer, editor and translator, and a business owner working to support the autonomy of tiny scale tea farmers in Asia through education, direct trade, and creative partnerships. I am driven and energized by a core set of values: allyship and advocacy, generosity, collective responsibility, and respect for others, their independence, and their capacities. I have entered the field of web development because I believe software can dismantle all sorts of barriers, including those that limit access to knowledge, to resources, and to our fellow humans. I am seeking a professional situation where I can face challenging and meaningful projects with a team. I want to use code to help produce new possibilities for thinking, understanding, and living.
