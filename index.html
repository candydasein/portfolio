Michael Loren Siegel

Solutions Architect, Communicator, Developer

I am a customer-centric, technically advanced solutions architect and full stack web developer with a strong understanding of software products and a background in higher education, strategic communications, and social entrepreneurship. I love nothing more than solving complex problems, learning and growing, making connections between people and technologies, and helping others succeed.

I have almost 20 years of professional experience to date (see below). Before moving into tech, I previously worked as a professor and scholar of film and digital media studies, a higher ed communications strategist and institutional reformer in the area of diversity, inclusion, and equity, and a writer, editor and translator. I am, in addition, an officially certified Tea Master, and I own a small tea import and sales business called Three Mouths Tea which supports the autonomy of tiny scale tea farmers in Asia through education, direct trade, and creative partnerships.

I am driven and energized by a set of core values: allyship and advocacy, generosity, collective responsibility, and respect for others, their needs, their independence, and their capabilities. Technology helps me realize and activate these values. I believe that software can dismantle all sorts of barriers, including those that restrict the access that we can gain to knowledge, to resources, and to our fellow humans.

I excel at uniting business, personal, and technical needs, conceiving and realizing large-scale projects, working collaboratively, diagnosing complex problems and offering solutions, communicating across boundaries, thinking creatively but in a structured, logical way, and isolating, acknowledging, and enhancing others' strengths.
